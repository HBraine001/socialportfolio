Creator Partnership Content Series Community

Scump × SCUF — Signature Controller

↗
Overview

Led content strategy for a signature controller launch with legendary Call of Duty pro Scump. Rather than executing a single hero launch video, the approach was to build a high-volume content ecosystem, including unboxings, interviews, and supporting pieces, designed to extend the cultural moment across multiple touchpoints and maximize audience engagement over time.

The strategy proved that sustained creator content outperforms one-time drops, driving strong engagement rates well above industry benchmarks.

"Instead of one launch moment, we built a content world around it. Giving fans multiple reasons to engage, share, and come back."
